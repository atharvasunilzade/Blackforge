// Our Story

Built in Akola.
Built for India.

We started BlackForgeX because we saw Vidarbha businesses struggling to access quality tech. We're changing that.

// The Beginning

Why We Started

Akola and Vidarbha have some of India's most hardworking entrepreneurs — farmers, manufacturers, traders, and service providers who deserve access to the same technology as Mumbai or Bengaluru companies.

We saw too many businesses paying ₹5 lakh+ to Pune agencies who didn't understand local needs, or struggling with off-the-shelf software that didn't fit their workflow. So we started BlackForgeX to change that.

Founded in February 2026, we are a team of passionate engineers committed to building world-class software right here in Akola — at prices that make sense for Indian businesses.
