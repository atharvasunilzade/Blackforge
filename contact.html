FAQ

How long does a project take?

Simple websites take 2–4 weeks. Custom software typically 6–12 weeks. Mobile apps 8–14 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline after scoping.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely. We love early-stage startups. We offer flexible payment milestones and can start with an MVP to keep initial costs low.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes. All projects include 1–12 months of post-launch support depending on the package. We also offer annual AMC contracts.

Who owns the code?

You do. Full source code ownership transfers to you on final payment. No lock-in, no licenses — it's completely yours.

Can you work remotely?

Yes. We work with clients across India remotely via video calls, Slack, and project dashboards. Local clients can also visit our Akola office.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, bank transfer, NEFT/RTGS, credit/debit cards, and cheques. We typically work in milestones — 40% advance, 40% mid, 20% on delivery.
